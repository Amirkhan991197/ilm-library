About Ilm Library

Ilm Library provides a peaceful and silent study environment where students can focus on their studies without any disturbance. Students can study comfortably with their books and laptops.

We offer comfortable seating, proper lighting, WiFi, power backup, fully air-conditioned rooms, RO drinking water, and CCTV security. The library is open 24√ó7 and also provides newspapers and current affairs magazines to help students stay updated and achieve their goals.
